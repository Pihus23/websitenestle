About

Today, Nestlé is focused on producing products that enhance the quality of life and grnts a healthier lifestyle. The company has around 275,000 employees, over 2,000 brands, and products sold in 188 countries. Nestlé also has partnered with and created many well known brands such as KitKat, Nescafé, Maggi, Toll House, and much more. Nestlé research and test new ideas as quickly as they can with the goal of producing products that are good for the environment, tasty, and affordable. The dairy industry is where Nestlé first started and is still the most popular component of it. Their dairy products are well known to many people and are loved by people of all ages.
